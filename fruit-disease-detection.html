// About the Project

Built with Precision

A capstone-level deep learning research project applying computer vision to agricultural disease management.

  • 🎯
    Problem Statement

    Fruit diseases cause 20–40% of global crop losses annually. Early detection is critical — our system reduces identification time from days to seconds.

  • 🔬
    Methodology

    Transfer learning using ResNet-50 pretrained on ImageNet, fine-tuned on PlantVillage with 80/10/10 train/val/test split and 5-fold cross-validation.

  • 📈
    Impact

    Designed for farmers, agricultural researchers, and agronomists. Works offline-capable and deployable as a mobile app for field use.
